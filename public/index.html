Presenting Mike, The Product Manager Who Prioritizes Customer Needs

Mike Brings Your Product Vision To Life By Refining Your Product Ideas, Creating Your Roadmaps, And analyzing User Feedback.

Mike guides your product's strategy by analyzing markets, collaborating on roadmaps, and prioritizing user-focused features. With agile methodologies and clear communication, he ensures your products succeed.

Mike identifies user needs and translates them into actionable insights

Mike saves you countless hours by analyzing feedback to uncover user needs . He provides instant lists of feature requests, popular features , and other product insights while also determining the frequency users mention each issue.

He creates visually appealing roadmaps for your engineering team.

By understanding how to prioritize features, Mike creates product roadmaps based on user needs . As your product plan evolve, Mike will automatically update the roadmap to reflect these changes and communicate them to the rest of the engineering team

Mike cuts down the time required for writing requirement documents and user stories by as much as 80%

Mike takes care of all your product roadmap documentation needs. Including PRDs, User personas, and strategy documents. With Mike , outlining requirements, breaking them down, capturing key decision point, and providing input becomes effortless and enjoyable.

Mike actively contributes as a team collaborator

Mike will share weekly insights with your team on Slack and collaborate with them to discover and prioritize user insights. Additionally, he will promptly draft user stories with acceptance criteria to create tickets in Jira or your preffered platform for tracking the development process.

He will Actively participate in your product meetings and customer interviews

By actively participating in your product meetings and customer interviews , Mike identifies crucial action items, compiles user research notes, and furnishes interview transcripts . This accelerates and clarifies your product decision-making process.
